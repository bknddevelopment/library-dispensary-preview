Chapter 1: Understanding Cannabinoids

Cannabis contains over 100 different cannabinoids, each with unique properties and potential benefits. The most well-known cannabinoids include:

  • THC (Tetrahydrocannabinol) - The primary psychoactive compound
  • CBD (Cannabidiol) - Non-psychoactive with therapeutic properties
  • CBG (Cannabigerol) - The "mother" cannabinoid
  • CBN (Cannabinol) - Known for sedative effects

Chapter 2: Indica vs Sativa vs Hybrid

Understanding the differences between cannabis varieties helps you choose the right experience:

  • Indica - Relaxing, body-focused effects, ideal for evening
  • Sativa - Uplifting, cerebral effects, great for daytime
  • Hybrid - Balanced effects combining both varieties

Chapter 3: Consumption Methods

Different consumption methods offer unique experiences and onset times:

  • Inhalation - Fast onset (5-10 minutes), 1-3 hour duration
  • Edibles - Slower onset (30-120 minutes), 4-8 hour duration
  • Tinctures - Moderate onset (15-45 minutes), flexible dosing
  • Topicals - Localized relief without psychoactive effects

Chapter 4: Safe & Responsible Use

Best practices for cannabis consumption:

  • Start low, go slow - especially with edibles
  • Store products safely away from children and pets
  • Never drive or operate machinery while impaired
  • Know your limits and respect them
